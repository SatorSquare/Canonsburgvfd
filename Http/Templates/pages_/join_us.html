Join Us

Become a Canonsburg Volunteer Fire Fighter

  We are always in need of new members (men or women are welcome). Anyone interested in learning more about membership is invited to visit us to speak to a member or call the department at {{PHONE_READABLE}} and leave a message. We train every Monday night from 7:00 pm to 9:00 pm at the fire department. Our department needs you and your community needs your help!

Help us continue to do the important work we do and bring your volunteer spirit to the team.

To Join You Must:

  • Attend training
  • Pass a medical physical
  • Pass a physical agility test

Benefits of Joining:

  • Life Insurance policy
  • Additional Health and Accident Coverage
  • An annual physical
  • A close-knit family of members to grow and serve with
  • Family events

  The alternative to a volunteer fire department is to have a total paid/career department, which could cost local taxpayers millions of dollars a year in new taxes. It is documented that as a whole, the Volunteer Fire Service in the Commonwealth saves local taxpayers over 10 billion dollars a year in local taxes.
