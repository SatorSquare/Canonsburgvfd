Community Involvement

We Care About Our Community

  We are heavily involved in the local community. In partnership with the North Strabane Fire Department, we visit various schools within the Canon-McMillan School District to educate students in grades K-5th on fire prevention. We also offer tours of the fire hall and open our doors several times a year for field trips by local daycares and scouting groups.

  A question that often comes up in the community is car seat inspections, which we do not do on site, but residents can call the Canonsburg Police Department at {{POLICE_PHONE_READABLE}} to schedule an inspection.

We work hand-in-hand with local media to spread awareness and education and report on incidents and emergencies.
